# CMHC Rental Market Survey Source Notes

- Official source name: CMHC Rental Market Survey Data Tables.
- Source webpage URL: https://www.cmhc-schl.gc.ca/professionals/housing-markets-data-and-research/housing-data/data-tables/rental-market/rental-market-report-data-tables
- Excel download URL used: https://assets.cmhc-schl.gc.ca/sites/cmhc/professional/housing-markets-data-and-research/housing-data-tables/rental-market/rental-market-report-data-tables/2025/rmr-canada-2025-en.xlsx?rev=11bbba5e-64a5-4dcd-a81f-7252c2b12537
- Download date: 2026-06-22
- Data frequency: Annual Rental Market Survey publication.
- Geographic coverage: Canada, provinces, and major centres. The official page lists the 2025 edition and editions back to 2019.
- Downloaded edition inspected: Canada, 2025, published December 11, 2025.
- Key fields expected: centre/geography names; vacancy rates; turnover rates; average two-bedroom rents; percentage change in average rent; rental condominium apartment universe; private apartment/RMS universe; quality indicators.
- Measures expected: vacancy rate, average rent, turnover rate, rental-unit universe count, bedroom type, dwelling/structure type.
- Known limitations, suppression symbols, or comparability concerns:
  - The downloaded workbook is a formatted Excel publication, not a tidy flat table.
  - Quality indicators are used: `a` excellent, `b` very good, `c` good, `d` poor/use with caution.
  - `**` means data suppressed.
  - Statistical significance markers include up/down arrows and dashes; some tables use `Delta` for significant differences between condominium and purpose-built rental apartment measures.
  - Several sheets note that 2025 and 2024 data are based on 2021 census geographic definitions.
  - Major centres generally refer to CMAs, with noted exceptions such as Charlottetown in the workbook.
  - The latest downloaded file provides the 2025 publication with 2024 and 2025 columns in core tables; it is not a long historical city panel without additional edition downloads.
